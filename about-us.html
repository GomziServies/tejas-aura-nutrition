Meet Mr. Tejas Kumar U. Katara,

The founder and CEO of AURA NUTRITION. A retired Indian Army personnel, he brings with him 24 years of rich experience in the fitness industry.

As a former wrestling athlete, he has always been deeply passionate about physical fitness, natural bodybuilding, and disciplined living. His journey in fitness began during his early years and continued throughout his military career, where physical endurance and strength were a way of life.

After retiring from the Indian Army in 2020, he observed a major gap in the fitness market—many hardworking gym-goers from lower and middle-class families were unable to afford pure and reliable nutrition supplements. This realization sparked a new mission: to create a trustworthy brand that offers affordable, genuine, and high-quality supplements for everyone.

That mission gave birth to AURA NUTRITION—a brand driven by passion, integrity, and accessibility. The goal is to empower individuals from all walks of life to achieve their fitness goals without financial constraints.

With AURA NUTRITION , Mr. Katara is making premium nutrition affordable and accessible—because everyone deserves the chance to transform their body and live a healthier life.

Elevate your fitness journey with AURA Nutrition's premium supplements for strength, muscle growth, and recovery.
